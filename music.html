Sewerslvt / Cynthoni / AgonyOST The first time I was introduced to Jvne, I told my friend to fuck off and called it trash. This was in freshman year of high school. I'm not sure when, but eventually my curiosity got the better of me and I had to investigate what my friend was so heavily preaching to me. Jvne's sound is something many will cast to the side as "just noise" or "bullshit". And that's a completely valid take. Many call her music "fake breakcore" and argue about what genre she falls into. Controversy aside, her music was very captivating to me at the time. Listening to Sewerslvt is like having your brain blasted with drums and symbals, drowning out any cohesive thoughts in your mind. Then, the synth melodies come in which resonate with a lot of people, including myself at one point. I still listen to some of her songs today. She now goes under a different moniker "Cynthoni" Cyberia Lyr1
